The RxLog system, developed by VGR, serves as a crucial tool for the ordering and storage of medication. Its primary purpose is to streamline the administrative processes associated with medication storage and distribution. The system is utilized by both Specialists and internal staff within VGR. It handles sensitive information, including personal data and health data. RxLog is hosted on-premise and is accessible exclusively via the internal network, ensuring restricted access. Notably, this system does not incorporate any artificial intelligence components. Its criticality is rated as Medium, indicating a potential operational impact if disrupted.
